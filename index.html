About Me

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. I am in Multimedia Laboratory (MMLab), NTU, supervised by Prof. Chen Change Loy. Before that, I received my bachelor’s degree from Jilin University in July 2019.
