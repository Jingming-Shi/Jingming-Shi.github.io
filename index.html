About Me

Pressure can change the interaction between atoms in a substance and induce the formation of high-pressure new phases, most of which have novel physical and chemical properties compare to the regular phases at ambient condition. I was working on high pressure and phases transitions since 2011 and got the Master degree in Jilin University. After that, I joined the University of Lyon to pursue a PhD and still engaged in theoretical simulations. After I got my Doctor degree of physics, I worked in Jiangsu Normal University and focus on exploration of novel crystal structures and new materials at high pressure, such as superconductors, high-energy density materials and superhard materials.
